Duquesne University Dashboard

Here is a dashboard to test out read/write permissions, licenses, and refreshing of data. You'll need to make a Power BI account to view, but hopefully you don't need a pro account. I gave read access to a few people and left some people off just for testing purposes.

To update the dashboard, we'll 1) append a .csv table onto an existing table on CIFS, 2) refresh the dashboard in the desktop version of PBI and publish to the online workspace. Everything will automatically update from there.

Step 1 will be manual until we establish what dashboards we want. Step 2 can be scheduled to update/refresh automatically.
