Cascio Research Group

Projects in the Cascio Lab

We do lots of interesting science in our lab. In particular, we focus on the waxing and waning of the moon and how it correlates to the number of Kit-Kat bars purchased in the Northern Mariana Islands. Using state-of-the-art instruments, such as the trebuchet, we have attained some very intriguing results.
